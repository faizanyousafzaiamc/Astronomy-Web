Observatories

Observatories are the windows through which humanity explores the universe. From the peaks of mountains to the depths of space,these incredible scientific facilities help us unlock the secrets of stars, planets, and galaxies.

Famous Observatories Around the World

Mauna Kea Observatory

Located on Hawaii's tallest mountain, it hosts some of the world's most powerful telescopes.

Arecibo Observatory

Once the world's largest radio telescope, it contributed to many key astronomical discoveries.

Palomar Observatory

Home to the famous Hale Telescope, an optical telescope with a 200-inch mirror.
