Earth's Atmosphere

The atmosphere is a dynamic shield of gases surrounding our planet, protecting life and shaping weather, climate, and communication.

Troposphere

Closest to Earth. Weather forms here. Temperature decreases with altitude.

Stratosphere

Houses the ozone layer. Protects us from harmful UV rays. Jets fly here.

Mesosphere

Coldest layer. Meteors burn up here due to atmospheric friction.

Thermosphere

Contains ionosphere. Northern lights occur here. Space stations orbit in this layer.

Exosphere

Outermost layer. Gradually merges into space. Very thin and light gases.
